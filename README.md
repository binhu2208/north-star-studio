## Executive Summary

Flicker is a minimalist roguelike where your torch is your life, your vision, and your weapon. Light burns in real-time — even while you plan your next move.

After 3 development sprints and 3 review cycles, the game has received **unanimous approval** from the review team.

---

## How to Play

1. Open `src/index.html` in any modern browser
2. Use **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** to move
3. Press **SPACE** to attack (costs 10% torch fuel)
4. Collect **⚡ fuel** to extend your torch
5. Find the **🚪 exit** to descend deeper
6. Avoid **👁️ The Stalker** — or fight it
7. Survive 3 levels to win

---

## Game Features

### Core Mechanics
- **Torch System:** Your only light source burns down over time
- **Combat:** Attack enemies at the cost of precious fuel
- **Procedural Dungeons:** Every run is different
- **3-Level Descent:** Escalating difficulty with each floor

### Progression
- **Upgrade System:** Choose between Speed, Efficiency, or Vision after each level
- **Victory Stats:** Track your time and fuel collection
- **Build Variety:** Different upgrade combinations create different playstyles

### Technical
- Single HTML file (~26KB)
- Zero dependencies
- Runs in any modern browser
- FPS-independent mechanics
