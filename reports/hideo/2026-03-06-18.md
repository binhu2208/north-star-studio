## Balance Observations

### Current Balance State: GOOD ✅

Based on Sakura's QA testing and my review:

**Torch Burn Rate:**
- Level 1: ~41 seconds base drain
- Level 2: +30% faster ( ~31 seconds)
- Level 3: +60% faster (~25 seconds)
- With efficiency upgrade: +30% duration

**This creates good tension escalation.**

**Combat Balance:**
- 10% per attack = ~10 attacks max
- In practice: 2-3 attacks per level
- Creates "save fuel or fight" decisions

**Enemy Difficulty:**
- Level 1: 800ms move interval (slow)
- Level 2: 680ms (15% faster)
- Level 3: 578ms (30% faster from base)
- Speed boost upgrade helps kite effectively

### Potential Tuning (Post-Launch)

| Aspect | Current | Consider | Reason |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|
| Bright Torch | +1 tile vision | +2 tiles or +0.5 damage | Still underwhelming per reviews |
| Speed Boost | +1 tile/move | Cap at +2? | Could become OP with stacking |
| Fuel spawn | 3-5 + level | Scale with map size? | Larger maps on L2/L3 need more fuel |
