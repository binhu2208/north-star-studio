### 3. ❌ Duplicate Project Folder
**Found:** `projects/flicker/` — Old duplicate of flicker-html content

**Action:** Removed entire `projects/` directory

### 4. ❌ Orphaned Root-Level Folders
**Found:**
- `scenes/` — Old Godot scenes (duplicates of flicker-godot/scenes/)

**Action:** Removed — current versions exist in `flicker-godot/`

### 5. ❌ Missing `flicker-assets/` at Root
**Found:** No shared asset folder at root level

**Action:** Created `flicker-assets/` with proper structure:
